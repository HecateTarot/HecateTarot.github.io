About Kyera

I was drawn to the Tarot in my early twenties when, freshly arrived in London and having left friends and family behind, I picked up a deck of cards (the Aleister Crowley one at the time) “just for fun”. Having been raised an atheist by anti-Church parents, I was then inherently very sceptical about it. However, very quickly, I became more and more intrigued by this deck and decided to start learning the Art properly.

Fast forward 20 years and a marvellous journey of self-discovery, reading for friends and relatives along the way. I decided to set up my business to share Light with and assist Querents.

I pride myself on working solely by word of mouth. Don't expect to see me in a Facebook ad! I draw on the rich and complex Tarot symbology, numerology, Eastern & Western philosophies to help you unveil your unconscious and explore what drew you to me, so as to assist you in your path for spiritual and emotional nourishment.

I am a member of the London College of Psychic Studies and have studied the Tarot according to the teachings of renowned clairvoyants and Tarot readers. I regularly attend courses and workshops to grow and develop along my Spiritual Path. 

When not practising this art, I enjoy playing the harp and the piano.

Let's look together beyond the veil! I very much look forward to meeting you.
